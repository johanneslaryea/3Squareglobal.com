FAQs

What services do you offer?
We offer a wide range of services including web development, mobile app development, digital marketing, and UI/UX design. Our team specializes in creating tailored solutions to meet your unique business needs.
How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. Here are some general guidelines:

• Simple website: 4-6 weeks

• Custom website or application: 8-16 weeks

• Complex enterprise application: 3-6 months or more.

We work closely with our clients to establish realistic timelines and milestones, ensuring we meet their needs and expectations.

How does the subscription model work?

Our tiered subscriptions offer varying project capacities and features. Choose a plan (Basic, Standard, or Pro) and request up to a certain number of projects at a time. You can submit unlimited tasks, including design, development, and marketing requests, with unlimited revisions. Turnaround times vary by project complexity, with simple tasks completed in 3 days and others within a week. Visit our "Pricing" page for more details.

How do I collaborate with my creative team?

1. Subscribe: Choose your package and enter company details and payment method.

2. Onboard: Join an onboarding video call to discuss expectations, meet your assigned team member and their manager.

3. Collaborate: Integrate your 3Square team into your company culture. Get to know them, provide training materials, and assign tasks to get started.

4. Relax: Our dedicated team takes ownership of their roles, striving for excellence and delivering exceptional results.

How is this better than freelancers or agencies?

- Convenience: Instant access to a dedicated team with a subscription, no need to interview or manage multiple freelancers.

- Cost-effective: Predictable monthly fees, no hourly rates or surprise agency costs.

- Scalability: Easily adjust project volume by upgrading or downgrading your plan.

- Streamlined Workflow: Collaborate effortlessly with your team on our platform, with a single point of contact (project manager).

- Unlimited Revisions: Get revisions until you're satisfied, no extra charges.

Can I access and edit the final design files?

Yes, upon payment completion, you'll receive ownership rights to use approved design and writing work.

You'll also get access to editable design files, enabling you to:

  • Make minor tweaks independently
  • Share with designers/developers for further implementation

Note: 3Square Global reserves the right to re-use creative work rejected or not approved by the client.
